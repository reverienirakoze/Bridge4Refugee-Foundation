Where
A Refugee
Regains Balance

For many, becoming a REFUGEE feels
like the end — but with a second chance,
they can REWRITE their story and turn
the impossible into possible.

OUR MISSION

Bridge4Refugee empowers displaced communities
by providing access to education, technology,
and creative platforms that amplify their voices
and inspire change.

OUR VISION

A world where every refugee has the opportunity
to thrive, contribute...

Did You Know?

Due to funding cuts, many refugee students in Rwanda,
including 553 students qualified for boarding schools,
were unable to attend due to a lack of resources.

Did You Know?

Over 60,000 refugee students in Rwanda face the risk of
repeating classes due to the termination of the automatic promotion policy,
leading to a rise in dropout rates.

Did You Know?

Only 21% of refugee children attend secondary school in Rwanda,
with even fewer pursuing higher education.
